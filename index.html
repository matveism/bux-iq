Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points?

Complete surveys, offers, and tasks in the Earn section to accumulate points that can be redeemed for rewards.

How can I redeem rewards?

Once you've accumulated enough points, visit the Shop section to select and redeem your preferred reward.

What are penalty points?

Penalty points are added at **1 point per instance** when bonus codes are abused or incorrect Account IDs are entered. Only correct Account IDs should be used.

Yes, penalty points can be removed. Each point costs **250 points** to remove from your balance. To remove **2 penalty points**, it will cost **500 points**. If you accumulate **3 penalty points**, your account will be blocked, meaning you won't be able to use it. To remove all 3 points once this limit is reached, it will cost **938 points**, which includes a 25% penalty fee.
